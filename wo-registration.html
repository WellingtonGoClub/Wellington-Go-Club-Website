Tournament conditions

  • The tournament will have 7 main rounds. Four on Saturday and three on Sunday.
  • New Zealand rules will be used. Breaking the rules will result in immediate loss of the game and possible disqualification from the tournament.
  • Byo-yomi time control will be used, with 10 minutes main time and 3 byo-yomi periods of 30 seconds
  • Tie-breaker games will be used to determine an ultimate winner in the case of a first place tie. Tie-breaker games will use New Zealand rules, with a byo-yomi of 1 minute main time and 1 byo-yomi period of 10 seconds.
